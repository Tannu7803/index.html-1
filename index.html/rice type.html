Various Type of Rice

Basmati Rice

Basmati is a long-grain rice known for its fragrance and is commonly used in biryanis and pilafs.

1121 (Golden stream) Rice

1121 is a variant of Basmati known for itsxtra-long grain.it is highly aromatic and used in premium dishes.

1718 stream/sella Rice

1718 stream/sella Rice is incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of dishes including-pilafs,biryanis,fried rice,desserts,risottos.

Pr-14 sella/ stream Rice

Pr-14 sella/stream Rice is a type of polishd parboiled rice that hs had the bran layer stripped away.Due to this treatment,the rice is made white and has a glossy texture.
